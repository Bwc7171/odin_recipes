Lemon Garlic Chicken

Description

Lemon Garlic Chicken is a great way to turn ordinary chicken into an all time favorite meal. One can simply use simple ingredients such as lemons, chicken, garlic and a few fresh herbs to make this tasty entree.

Ingredients

  • 4 lemons
  • 2-3 heads of garlic
  • 1 whole chicken
  • Fresh Rosemary or your favorite fresh herbs
  • All purpose steak seasoning or salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cut garlic cloves and lemons in half.
  2. Place garlic and lemons in bottom of slow cooker.
  3. Clean and Rinse chicken. Make sure to pat the chicken dry.
  4. Place the chicken into the crock pot.
  5. Cover the top of chicken with lemon slices
  6. Stuff the chicken with garlic head cut in half and a lemon cut into quarters.
  7. Cover the chicken with additional slices of chicken and rosemary.
  8. Turn slow cooker to high and cook for about 4 hours, or until chicken reaches 165 degrees internally.
  9. Remove from slow cooker, carve and serve.
