Meatloaf

Description

Meatloaf is a simple entree that only requires a few simple ingredients. In addition, meatloaf is can be prepared and served quickly and easily.

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1 small onion
  • milk
  • 1 bag bread crumbs
  • salt and pepper
  • ketchup

Steps

  1. Mix the gound beef with the bread crumbs, egg and milk
  2. Chop the onion and mix with the other ingredients
  3. Mix the previous ingredients with 1 1/2 cup of ketchup.
  4. Place the ingredients in a loaf pan and bake at 350 degrees for an hour.
  5. Slice and serve the meatloaf.
