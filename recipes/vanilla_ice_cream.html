Vanilla Ice Cream

Description

Vanilla Ice cream is an all time favorite and sure crowd pleaser to serve at the end of just about any meal. The ingredients are usually readily available and preparation is fairly simple. The even beter selling point for this recipe is that one can add a plethora of toppings to enhance the experience.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 2 cups half and half
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients, stirring to dissolve sugar completely.
  2. Fill cylinder of ice cream churn no more than two-thirds full.
  3. Freeze according to manufacturer's directions. Serve immediately or store in a covered container in freezer.
